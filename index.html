Newington Football Club

We are a NIFL Premier Intermidiate Division football club from Belfast.

Our senior team competes in the NIFL Premier Intermediate Division; playing our home games at Solitude (Google Maps). Our U20 development squad play their games in the NIFL Championship Development League, while Newington's youth set-up provides footballing opportunities to the area’s young people.
