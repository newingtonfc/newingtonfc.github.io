Newington Football Club

Newington FC is an Irish League football club from Belfast, Ireland.

Our first team competes in the NIFL Premier Intermediate Division; playing our home games at Seaview. Our reserve team play in the Ballymena & Provincial League’s Junior Division 1. Our U20s play their games in the NIFL Development League, while Newington's youth set-up provides footballing opportunities to the area’s young people.
